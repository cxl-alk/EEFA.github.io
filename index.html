Our Purpose

The EEFA is not an organization, but rather a slogan meant to unite parents and caretakers of students to defend our education system. As of right now, according to USNews, Virginia is top 10 in education in the U.S. However, this position is currently threatened by officials and candidates who want to change our schools. It is our duty to stand up to these threats protect our childrens future.

  • The Problem

The Current Issues

The Trump Administration is threatening Virginia’s school by cutting our school spending budget in half. With Ed Gillespie, running for governor he is almost certain to comply with his fellow Republican and sign the legislation. Ed also plans to give “a tax giveaway for upper-income Virginians who send their children to private schools...”, as well as more funding for Charter Schools and reduce public school spendings.
